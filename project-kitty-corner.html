Kitty's Corner Renovations

We're thrilled to announce our successful bid for a commercial renovation project situated in Lexington, South Carolina. The building's previous identity as a Golden Corral Restaurant is about to undergo a remarkable transformation. This revitalization encompasses a comprehensive exterior facelift, highlighted by the addition of new parapet towers, fresh siding, upgraded masonry, modern canopies, and sleek storefronts, all meticulously designed to breathe new life into the structure.

The interior of the building will see a complete overhaul as well, as it gets demolished and reconfigured into three distinct, separately metered tenant spaces. This reimagining of the interior layout is aimed at accommodating diverse businesses and enhancing the building's functionality. Simultaneously, the parking lot will undergo extensive repairs, including the removal of a driveway access point, the reconfiguration of curbs, the revitalization of sidewalks, and the essential milling and repaving of the existing parking area. This comprehensive project promises to not only rejuvenate the building but also revitalize the surrounding area, contributing to the vibrancy of the Lexington community.
