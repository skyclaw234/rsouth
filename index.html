Forging a Path in Building: The Rockwell South Story

Although not founded in Rockwell, North Carolina, the roots of Rockwell South's founder can be traced back to this small town. It was from these humble beginnings that the founder's journey eventually led to the company's establishment in the bustling landscape of South Carolina's Upstate. This relocation strategically positioned the company closer to a broader client base and a host of new projects, fostering its growth and development.
